Clear Answers to your Questions, Ensuring
a Smooth Design Journey

1. What is the process for working with you?

The process begins with an initial consultation where we discuss your project goals. I then provide design concepts, followed by revisions based on your feedback. Once the final design is approved, I deliver all the necessary assets.

2. How do I know which plan is right for my project?

I offer different packages tailored to various needs and budgets. During our initial consultation, I’ll learn more about your goals and help you choose the plan that aligns best with your project scope and timeline.

3. Can I make changes to the design after the final delivery?

Yes! I understand that small updates might be needed. Each plan includes a specific number of post-delivery revisions. Any additional changes beyond that can be arranged for a small fee.

4. What’s included in the prototyping service?

The prototyping service includes interactive mockups that simulate the user experience and flow of your final design. You’ll be able to click through the layout just like a real app or website, helping you validate and approve functionality before development.

5. How long will it take to complete my project?

Timelines vary depending on the complexity and scope of your project. Most projects are completed within 1 to 3 weeks. After our initial call, I’ll give you a clear timeline with milestones and delivery dates.

6. What formats will I receive my final design in?

You will receive all necessary assets in high-resolution and industry-standard formats, such as PNG, SVG, PDF, and editable Figma files. I ensure you get everything needed for development, print, or web use.
